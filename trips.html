Trips

View more...

The society runs a wide variety of trips throughout the year. We like to go all over the country, to some of the most beautiful areas the UK has to offer. Trip destinations include the Yorkshire Dales, the Peak District, the Lake District, Scotland, Snowdonia and more. On these trips you can usually find a wide array of activities to choose from, often including: climbing, caving, walking, ghyll scrambling and sometimes skills training. For example we have run a trip to the Yorkshire Dales for members to undertake a First Aid course.
