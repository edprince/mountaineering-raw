Climbing

If you are interested in climbing, or are keen to start, then you won't be disappointed at this club. As well running frequent trips to climb in some of the most beautiful parts of the country, we also run weekly trips to the climbing wall at the Warwick University Sports Centre. Check out our equipment page for our recommended equipment for climbing.

Important Information

  • You must take the 12X or 11U buses to get to Warwick University from Coventry city centre (12X will save you 10 minutes).
    • 12X Timetables
    • 11U Timetables
  • You must bring £4 in exact change to cover the cost of the return bus ticket.
  • Without a membership, climbing sessions cost £6, and you can stay as long as you wish.
  • If you wish to hire climbing shoes, you can do so for a price of £3.50 at the centre.
  • The club has harness's and belay devices that you can borrow for free.

Hiking

As a club we are very keen on simply walking in beautiful areas. Most of our trips away will provide an option for walking, and most will have a variety of walks, depending on what you fancy. Anything from a gentle stroll with a pub lunch, to scaling vast mountains, bringing stunning views. We have also been fortunate enough to have had the opportunity for outdoor workshops, with outdoor experts. These have included learning many navigational skills.
